Fortnite: Save the World

Fortnite: Save the World is a cooperative shooter-survival game for up to four players to fight off zombie-like creatures and defend objects with fortifications they can build.

Fortnite Battle Royale

Fortnite Battle Royale is a free-to-play battle royale game where up to 100 players fight to be the last person standing.

Fortnite Creative

Fortnite Creative is a sandbox game mode where players are given complete freedom to create worlds and battle arenas.
